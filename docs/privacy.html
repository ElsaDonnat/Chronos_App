Privacy Policy

Chronos - Learn the Story of Humanity

Chronos does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Everything stays on your device.

Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device using your browser's localStorage. This includes:

  • Lesson progress and completion status
  • Event mastery scores
  • XP totals and streak counts
  • Starred/favorited events
  • App preferences

This data never leaves your device. There is no server, no cloud sync, and no backup service.

Data Collection

Chronos collects absolutely nothing. Specifically:

  • No personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • No usage analytics or tracking
  • No crash reports sent externally
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No cookies or fingerprinting
  • No location data

Network Requests

Chronos makes zero network requests. The app is fully offline-capable. All content, fonts, and assets are bundled within the app. No internet connection is required or used.

Third-Party Services

Chronos does not integrate with any third-party services, SDKs, or analytics platforms. There are no ads.

User Accounts

There are no user accounts, no sign-up, and no login. The app works entirely without authentication.

Data Deletion

You can delete all app data at any time by using the "Reset Progress" option in the app's Settings page, or by uninstalling the app. Since no data is stored externally, uninstalling removes everything permanently.

Children's Privacy

Chronos is an educational app suitable for all ages. Since no data is collected from anyone, no special provisions for children are necessary.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL. Since the app collects no data, meaningful changes are unlikely.
